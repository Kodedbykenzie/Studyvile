Project management

The business process of planning, developing, launching, and managing a product or service is known as product management. It encompasses a product's entire lifecycle, from concept to development to market launch. Product managers are in charge of ensuring that a product meets the needs of its target market and contributes to the business strategy, as well as managing a product or products throughout the product lifecycle.
